Popular Universities

Some of the popular universities of the twin cities.

National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences

FAST-NUCES

The National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, also commonly known as FAST, is a private university in Pakistan. It has five campuses based in different cities and was the first multi-campus university in Pakistan.

National University of Sciences and Technology

NUST

NUST School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, formerly NUST Institute of Information Technology, is a constituent school located in Islamabad, Pakistan. NUST-SEECS was launched on self-finance basis in April 1999 as a constituent college of National University of Sciences and Technology, Pakistan.

COMSATS University Islamabad

CUI

The COMSATS University Islamabad is a public university in Pakistan. It is a multi-campus university with its principal seat located in Islamabad.The university was established by the Commission on Science and Technology for Sustainable Development in the South (COMSATS) in 1998.
